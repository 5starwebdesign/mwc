Events

ANNIVERSARIES, BIRTHDAYS, DINNERS AND MORE

Make your special event memorable, when you book one of our luxury vehicles. Enjoy first class, door to door service as our professional chauffeur arrives in the vehicle you choose and takes you to your event in style.

CONCERTS

Rock the night from the start by booking your concert transportation with us. We offer a wide variety of concert packages for small or large groups. Private SUV’s, stretch limousines, stretch SUV’s, Party or Coach buses are all available at affordable prices.

SPORTING EVENTS

Do you have tickets for the big game? Midwest Coach Limousine can provide you with safe, reliable transportation. We offer fantastic tailgating packages at most stadiums. Our point to point sports package allows your stay for that extra inning or overtime.
